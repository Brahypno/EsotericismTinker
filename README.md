# EsotericismTinker

**Esotericism Tinker** is an add-on Mod For tinkers construct built around **Tinkers' Construct**, esoteric crafting,
alchemical transmutation, lunar structures, and cross-mod magical recipes.

It adds another layer of material handling, ritual structure, and tool modification,
allowing tools and materials to be changed through Esotericism process.

## Main Features

### Alchemy: Transmute

The transmutation system is built around the **Transmute**. Players can assemble a multiblock structure from
Ashen Blocks, then adjust its behavior through the controller, tanks, drains, ducts, heaters, and switches.

The Transmute Furnace inherits some familiar uses from smelteries and foundries, such as melting materials, storing
fluids, and processing ores, but it is not simply another smeltery. It has its own structure requirements, capacity
rules, and byproduct logic. In particular, the primary and secondary outputs of ores may be redistributed in ways that
differ from ordinary melting.

As the structure grows, the Transmute Furnace can hold more material, while its behavior is also affected by size, fuel
efficiency, melting mode, and alloying switches. It is a conversion structure made from Ashen Blocks, used to change how
materials are melted, stored, extracted, and redistributed.

### Astrology: Selenic

The selenic system is centered on the **Selenic Astrolabe**, a lunar structure composed of the Lunar Font, Armillary
Crown, Astrolabe Spine, and Testimony Stands.

Unlike ordinary crafting blocks, the Selenic Astrolabe depends on structure, position, input, medium, testimony,
elevation, and moon phase. Some recipes may only work at night and under specific lunar phases, or within certain height
ranges.

### Mod Compact

Esotericism Tinker also adds compact recipes for other magic and tool systems.

One of its major compact is an Ars Nouveau method for applying modifiers to Tinkers' Construct tools
. Instead of using only the normal Tinkers' Construct work station, some modifiers can be applied through Enchanting
Apparatus
recipes, giving modifiable tools another route of progression.

## In-Game Guide

The mod includes the **Hypnagogic Transmute** book, which documents its main systems in-game.

The guide covers:

* transmutation structures;
* Ashen Blocks and related components;
* tanks, drains, ducts, heaters, and switches;
* selenic structure assembly;
* Lunar Font, Armillary Crown, Astrolabe Spine, and Testimony Stand behavior;
* lunar recipe requirements;
* cross-mod tool modification recipes.

JEI support is also provided for supported recipe types where applicable.

## Design Direction

Esotericism Tinker focuses on three linked ideas:

* materials can be changed through controlled transmutation;
* tools can grow through more than ordinary modification tables;
* recipes can depend on structure, environment, and symbolic conditions.

## Notes

This mod is under active development. Features, recipes, structures, and balance may change between versions.
